Abstract

ONLINE FOOD ORDER is a website designed primarily for use in the food delivery industry. This system will allow hotels and restaurants to increase scope of business by reducing the labor cost involved. My system will allow restaurants to quickly and easily manage an online menu which customers can browse and use to place orders with just a few clicks.

Introduction

Online Ordering System can be defined as a simple and convenient way for customers to purchase food online, without having to go to the restaurant. This system is enabled by the internet – it is the internet that connects the restaurant or the food company on one hand, and the customer on other hand.
